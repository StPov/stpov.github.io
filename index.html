News Test App for CDG

Current application supports news search not only by certain channel, but also by keywords, providing best user experience. Also user has ability to save most appreciated news in app database.

Feature One

News search through channel supported by awesome UI with amazing animation.

Feature Two

News search by keyword is designed in Material Design style providing separate card with its elevation for each news article.

Feature Three

Saving the best news article on your phone is supported by Realm database.
